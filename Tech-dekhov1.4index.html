Our Standard

The brutal truth about tech you weren't supposed to know.

Spec sheets are written by marketing teams. We parse real-world benchmarks, strip the lab conditions from battery claims, and cross-reference community data from thousands of real users — without a single affiliate link clouding our judgment.

Real-World Battery Testing
We discard the manufacturer's video-playback claims. Our battery figures reflect actual mixed workloads: browsers open, Zoom running, code compiling. How we test →
Thermal & Structural Analysis
We flag chassis flex, throttling behaviour under sustained load, and poor hinge construction — the issues that surface six months after purchase when warranties stop mattering.
Soldered RAM & Upgrade Traps
Over 70% of modern laptops have completely non-upgradeable memory. We flag every soldered configuration so you know what you're locking yourself into at purchase time.
Zero Paid Placements
TechDekho earns nothing from brands or retailers. Our algorithm ranks purely on your specifications vs available hardware. No sponsored positions. Read our promise →

Most modern thin devices (especially Apple, Samsung, and many Dell/ASUS models) permanently solder the RAM to the motherboard during manufacturing. This means you can never upgrade it later — what you buy is what you're stuck with for the laptop's lifetime. This is a critical purchasing decision, especially for long-term use, and most reviewers bury or omit this fact entirely.
Our battery figures represent real mixed-use scenarios: web browsing, document editing, occasional video calls, with screen brightness around 150 nits. Manufacturer figures often use single-task video playback at minimum brightness in ideal temperature conditions — sometimes 2-3x more optimistic than real-world usage. Pre-written Investment Summary:

Raw Performance

Multi-threaded CPU benchmarks under real thermal conditions — not boost clocks that only last for 30 seconds.

Evaluation Metrics

  • Cinebench multi-core sustained
  • Compile time benchmarks
  • GPU rendering throughput

Battery Endurance

Measured at mixed workloads with screens at 150 nits — not video playback at minimum brightness.

Evaluation Metrics

  • Browser + productivity mix
  • Code compilation cycles
  • Light gaming discharge rate

Display Quality

Color accuracy in real viewing conditions. We flag panels that claim sRGB coverage but deliver muddy, inaccurate colors in practice.

Evaluation Metrics

  • sRGB and DCI-P3 coverage
  • Brightness at max nits
  • Refresh rate consistency

Value for Money

What do you actually get per rupee spent? We compare specs, build quality, longevity expectations, and India street prices.

Evaluation Metrics

  • Street price vs. spec ratio
  • Repairability score
  • Long-term upgrade potential

Build Quality

Physical chassis stress tests, hinge durability, flex measurements, and keyboard travel depth — the stuff that breaks after 8 months.

Evaluation Metrics

  • Chassis flex rating
  • Hinge stability score
  • Keyboard travel depth

Portability

Weight, thickness, charger size, and whether you'll actually want to take it to class every day for 4 years.

Evaluation Metrics

  • Total weight with charger
  • Thickness at thickest point
  • Bag-friendliness score
